What Is E‑commerce Summit

2019

The first e‑commerce summit in Egypt returns this year to drive the growth of e‑commerce in Egypt & the MENA region!

A grass-root movement to stir up the conversation on the potential of selling online, a place to educate & raise awareness on the inner workings of e‑commerce & a unique chance for top-tier players to network with different service providers & industry leaders, driving the growth of e‑commerce in the MENA region.

It’s a first of its kind opportunity for every E‑Commerce-related function to be present under one roof raising awareness about E‑Commerce.

E‑Commerce Summit will introduce you to the wholesome E‑Commerce experience and its inner workings from stepping into selling online and tailoring your business strategy to handling payments and logistics, understanding what your customers need and how to shape the experience to achieve conversion as well as all the behind-the-scenes technicalities.

1 Day. 3 Tracks

Strategy Track

Understand the consumer needs and wants, the importance research and how data is valuable for E‑Commerce to go forward and get a glimpse of the future of E‑Commerce.

Tactics Track

Explore what you need to transform your business to sell online, how to define your long & short-term business goals, how your organization needs to change and what about the local and international markets? All of this and more in the Strategy Track.

Execution Track

Discover all the nitty gritties your business needs to be investing in and watch and listen to the stories of businesses that transformed online and how can you follow in their suits.
